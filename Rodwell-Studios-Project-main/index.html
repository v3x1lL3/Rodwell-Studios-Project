WELCOME TO RODWELL STUDIOS HOME PAGE

Rodwell Studios is an organization consisting of 4 students in Ateneo de Davao University who are passionate about digital art.

As artists, they believe that art is an expression in which individuals should desire and be inspired about. Furthermore, this

can engage in activities such as creativeness and sharing ideas to each other, while achieving goals in general. These digital

artists can help motivate people who are into digital arts as well, remembering its history and understanding why it is important.
